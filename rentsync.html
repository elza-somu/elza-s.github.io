Property Management Website

The Requirement

The objective was to create a web pages for the property management company’s website, CenterPM

Context

  • Project Type — Individual
  • Role — Research, Ideation, UI/UX
  • Tools— Figma, Adobe XD, Google Slides

The Process

Ideal Solution

The ideal solution in this scenario would ensure that a user is able to find the desired property and get queries answered in the quickest possible time to enable this.

See solution

Discover Phase

During the Discover Phase, extensive user interviews and the market study was conducted to understand why a user struggles to find properties.

To comprehend this the steps that were undertaken were:

  • Market Research
  • User Interview
Market Research

Knowing the market is pivotal, as it aides in finalizing the Unique Selling Proposition

While most of the Direct Competition to "CenterPM" use websites which are text heavy and sometimes outdated.

Indirect Competitors excel at creating mordern, intutive websites that connect with their users.

User Interview

To understand the users, Interviews & Surveys were conducted. And when analysing the data collected, came to the conclusion that users are looking for “ease of navigation” and “communication”

Define Phase

User Persona

To reach our target users, persona was created and pain points were identified.

Develop Phase

User Journey

Tom's journey through the site was then defined.

Ideation
Creating a WireFrame & Usibility testing

Using the finding in interviews, persona and user journey, wireframe was created to reach the final product incorporating the branding for the company.

Once a wireframe was created, Usibility testing was conducted to understand if users found the movement through the website intutive.

Deliver Phase

Visual Design

Using the feedbacks during usability testing and findings from the user interviews, one of the main features that was added was a Chat Bot. It would essentially help users get their enquires answered quickly, which was one of the main problem areas.

Assumptions & Constraints

  1. The Design system had to stay on point with exsisting branding
  2. The prototype is developed as desktop first.
  3. The main objective is to drive customers to the platform.
  4. This study is conducted from the point of view of the customer as opposed to property owners.
