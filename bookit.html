BookIt

The Opportunity

To create a platform that provides easy access to services for the Hotel A guests. The hotel's primary pain point is that guests cannot communicate their requirements with the hotel staff, thus causing a drop in hotel ratings.

Context

  • Project Type — Individual
  • Role — Research, Ideation, UI/UX
  • Tools— Adobe XD, Photoshop, Figma, Google Slides
  • Timeline — February to April 2021

Research Phase

The client goals are condensed into a few key ones.

  • Social — to be accessible
  • Accuracy — Easier for hotels to understand customer requests and minimise miscommunication
  • Support Local Businesses

During the industry analysis, it was found that the currently available apps only targeted at booking hotels, restaurants and travels, and none was available for "in-hotel services".

Similar Apps
  • Handy — This partnering innovation is not just an app, but also a service that provides hotels with guest-ready smartphones loaded with in-room technology.
  • Inspirock — a mobile site that works like an app to generate a custom itinerary that suits the guest’s preferences.
  • Whistle — A communication tool that allows hotels and guests to communicate.

Define Phase

Develop Phase

Ideation

After the initial study, the structure of the app started to take shape. The app would facilitate two-way communication between the hotel staff and customers via chat. The services range from room service, book pool facilities or supplies with just a few clicks. This would also prove to be especially useful for people with anxiety, any disabilities in communication or introverted.

Some of the advantages of these features would be that-

  • Give the customer the ability to request services easily and efficiently
  • Allows for fewer miscommunications that might occur during a call-in
  • Makes recommendations of places that are in line with the customer’s needs

Next Steps

I would love to spend more time developing this project, in particular, to enable people with vision impairment to be able to use this app with ease.

This app can link up with other services such as travel packages, restaurant booking etc which can partner with the hotel and thus bring a more wholesome experience for the guests. Another option to generate revenue is by building a community for the premium members. This can help them to develop their virtual area to share experiences, recommendations, and brand loyalty.
