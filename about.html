Hi, There!

I am Elza, so glad that we could meet here.

I am a UX Designer who likes to create experiences for people. My journey into UX started quite early in my career when I realised I tend to advocate for my users and ensure that the design presented was in tune with their needs.

Once I took a deep dive into UX, I found that my problem solving and creative thinking worked perfectly with the role.

I am more than just a designer, and I strive to be a storyteller. I aim to make innovation more accessible, simple and easily understood by my users. My certifications in design and experience working with various UX strategies have granted me the opportunity to meet business goals proactively by designing experiences that are attractive, easy-to-use, enjoyable, and, most importantly, useful.
